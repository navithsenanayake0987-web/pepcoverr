Our scoring system

What the scores mean

Two independent scores. Neither replaces the other.

🔬

Science Score

Rates the quality of published research. A low score doesn't mean the peptide doesn't work — it means we don't have enough human data yet to know.

Strong — multiple human RCTs with large sample sizes
Moderate — small human trials or mixed results
Limited — animal studies only or very few humans tested
👥

Community Score

Aggregated from thousands of Reddit posts and forum reports. Shows what percentage of users report positive outcomes — broken down by use case.

Positive — user reported benefit or improvement
Mixed — partial benefit or inconsistent results
Negative — no benefit or side effects reported
